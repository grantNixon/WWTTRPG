  • Philosophies have been re-worked to Talents
  • Backgrounds
    • Now they only give flat skill bonuses. Their associated perks have been removed and moved into other aspects.
  • Skill Perks:
    • Cooking:
      • "Quick Snack" perk has been replaced with "Fight Prep" perk
    • Ropework:
      • "Knot Expert" perk has been replaced with "Disarm" perk
    • Number Adjustments:
      • "Paralyzing Strike" perk save has been reduced from 17 to 15
  • Armors/Clothes:
    • All now have upgraded versions that get stronger with each tier
  • Spells:
    • Most spells now branch at tier 2, giving the player more versatility with their favorite spells
  • Food:
    • Meals have been updated. Instead of giving a larger flat health bonus than Snacks,
      they now grant different upgrades depending on the type of Meal, like bonus MB or Inspiration
    • New Items
  • Weapons, guns, tonics, and more!
